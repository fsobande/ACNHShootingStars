Animal Crossing Shooting Stars and Star Recipes Guide

Animal Crossing:New Horizons has a fun feature: shooting stars and meteor showers. If you hear a "ding" sound or happen to notice a little red owl named Celeste roaming your island, chances are you might have shooting stars. Just wait between the hours of 7PM to 4AM, tilt your right joystick up to look at the sky,and press "A" when you see a shooting star. The next morning, you will find star fragments scattered on your beach.


The only problem with these stars is that it is hard to find out what type of stars will end up on the beach, or the when to get the stars needed for Celeste's DIY recipes. Thus, here is a guide on stars and star DIY recipes.
